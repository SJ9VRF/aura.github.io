Alignment · Agents · Autonomy

Sentinel Alignment Lab

A research-driven agent framework for evaluating and shaping the behavior of advanced multimodal systems under high-stakes conditions. The project focuses on misalignment detection, failure-mode discovery, deceptive or goal-drifting behaviors, reflective self-correction, and structured oversight through multi-agent evaluation loops.

Designed to align closely with frontier safety and agents work, it combines agentic reasoning, evaluator agents, tool use, and robustness-focused testing to study how capable systems can remain steerable, reliable, and decision-safe in real-world environments.

Embodied Alignment · Agents · Autonomy

Embodied Alignment WorldMaker

An embodied AI system focused on alignment in multi-agent environments, where autonomous agents interact within shared physical and simulated worlds. The project explores how alignment emerges and fails when multiple agents coordinate, compete, and act over long horizons.

Combines multimodal world models spanning vision, language, 3D, and action with multi-agent reinforcement learning, enabling agents to reason about other agents, maintain coherent goals, and operate under alignment constraints in dynamic environments.

Designed to study embodied alignment, agent interaction dynamics, and decision reliability in settings where perception, action, and coordination are tightly coupled.
